About PFI

AZSIC, is an organization of Zambian students studying at university level in various institutions of learning across China. This is a highly recognized association by the Zambian embassy in Beijing and the Zambian ministry of higher education, loan and scholarship board. The association aims to make the lives of all Zambian students affiliated to it via membership easier in China so as to facilitate the complete physical, social, spiritual and most importantly intellectual development of the concerned students. It is the ONLY official representative of Zambian students in China. Membership is open to all Zambian students in China regardless of their sponsorship status be it private sponsorship or scholarship as well as Zambians who graduated from china and are still in china.
